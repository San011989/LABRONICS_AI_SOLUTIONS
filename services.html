local_library Digital Library Setup

1

School Level: Physics & Computer Science

For school students, the focus is on foundational visualization. The library would provide the expensive equipment that individual schools might lack.

science Physics

Stations for optics (lasers, lenses), electromagnetism kits, and mechanics (friction tracks, pendulums).

code Computer Science

Beyond just PCs, this would include "Physical Computing" stations using Micro:bits or Raspberry Pi to see how code affects the physical world.

2

ITI College: Vocational Mastery

The ITI section focuses on skills-based proficiency and safety. For Electronics, Electrical, and Computer streams, the library acts as a workshop.

electrical_services Electrical

Wiring simulation boards, motor winding stations, and transformer testing benches.

precision_manufacturing Electronics

Soldering stations, PCB etching areas, and basic troubleshooting with multimeters.

computer Computer (COPA)

Network cabling (crimping tools), hardware assembly/disassembly, and OS installation labs.

3

Engineering College: Innovation & Research

At the engineering level, the library moves into high-complexity analysis and prototyping.

memory Electronics & Electrical

Advanced Power Electronics labs, VLSI design tools, and high-end Oscilloscopes (DSOs). "Digital Twin" technology where students simulate power grids.

dns Computer Engineering

Dedicated servers for Cloud Computing experiments, AI/Machine Learning clusters, and Cybersecurity sandboxes.
